Apex Zero

Apex Zero is a fast-paced sandbox PvP game inspired by Gorilla Tag, where agility, skill, and creativity collide. Set in immersive arenas, players use momentum-based movement to run, jump, and climb their way through intense battles. Customize your style, master the terrain, and outmaneuver your opponents in chaotic free-for-alls or team-based modes. Built from the ground up as a Gorilla Tag fan game, Apex Zero blends parkour freedom with competitive VR combat, offering endless replayability and player-driven fun.
